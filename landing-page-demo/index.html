Straight answers

Questions owners ask

Will it really sound like a person?
Yes — try it bottom-right with your sound on. It speaks naturally, handles interruptions, and books a real appointment. If it ever gets stuck, it hands off to you.
Do I have to change my phone number?
No. It works with your existing number. Calls you can't get to are answered by the AI; you can have it back you up or take everything after hours — your call.
How fast can I be up and running?
Usually about a week. We load it with your services, pricing ranges, service area, and booking rules, then test it with you before it goes live.
What if it can't answer something?
It captures the details and routes the caller (or a text) straight to you. You never lose the lead — you just don't have to catch every call yourself.
Is this just for HVAC?
No — it's tuned per trade. This page is the HVAC version; plumbing, roofing, lawn care, pest control, cleaning, electrical, auto and more each get their own setup.
